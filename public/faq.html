General

❓ What is this platform?

This platform provides an overview of the most important tokens and markets on the Cronos network. Projects can submit a request for listing, and users can easily access market information and statistics.

❓ Which networks do you support?

Currently, we only support the Cronos network. More networks may be added in the future depending on demand and developments.

❓ What is the purpose of this platform?

Our goal is to provide a transparent and reliable overview of available tokens on the Cronos network, including real-time market analysis, liquidity data, and price changes.

Listings & Registrations

❓ How can I list my coin/token on the platform?

You can submit a request through the listing form on our website. Fill in the required information, and our team will review your application.

❓ What are the requirements for listing a token?

  • The token must be live on the Cronos network.
  • Basic details such as contract address, name, and official links must be accurate and verifiable.
  • Misleading or scam projects will not be accepted.

❓ How long does it take for a token to be approved?

We aim to review applications within 24-48 hours. However, this may take longer if we receive a high volume of requests.

❓ Can I list an exchange?

Yes! In addition to tokens, decentralized exchanges (DEXs) can also be listed on the platform. Use the listing form to submit your request.

Market Data & Prices

❓ Where does the market data come from?

Our real-time price and liquidity data is collected from trusted APIs, such as GeckoTerminal and various Cronos DEXs.

❓ Why does the market cap on the coin page differ from the one in the general list?

We use the most accurate data available, but sometimes certain APIs update market caps at different speeds. We always display the most reliable value we can obtain.

❓ Why is my token not in the top gainers or newcomers list?

Only the top 5 tokens based on recent price increases and the newest added tokens are displayed. If your token is missing, it could mean that other tokens had higher price increases or that your token was not recently listed.

Voting & Community Sentiment

❓ How does the voting process work?

Users can vote positive or negative for each token to create a community sentiment. This helps assess a token’s reputation.

❓ Can I vote multiple times per day?

No, you can only vote once every 24 hours per token to prevent spam and manipulation.

❓ How is community sentiment calculated?

Sentiment is based on the total number of positive and negative votes. We display the percentage breakdown in a visual bar on the token page.

Security & Reliability

❓ Are all tokens on this platform considered safe?

No. We do not provide investment advice, and a token’s presence on our platform does not mean it is a safe investment. Always do your own research before investing.

❓ Can scam tokens be removed?

Yes, if a token is reported as a scam and we receive sufficient evidence, we can remove it from our database. You can report suspicious projects via our support page.

❓ How is the data kept up to date?

Our data is automatically updated using multiple APIs and market analysis tools. We continuously monitor the reliability of this data.
