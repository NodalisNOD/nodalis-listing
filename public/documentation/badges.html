Each badge on Nodalis is earned through clear and transparent criteria. Below is a breakdown of how each badge can be obtained:

Innovative Project

  • Project introduces a new concept, technology, or use case in Web3.
  • Must include a whitepaper or technical writeup.
  • Community suggestions via form or ticket are considered.
  • Final decision made by Nodalis team after review.

  • Ranked in the top 5 based on highscore upvotes.
  • Minimum 100 unique votes.
  • Live tracking and automatic updates.

Top 10

  • Ranked in the top 10 on the leaderboard.
  • Minimum 50 unique votes required.

Top Performer (24h)

  • Highest 24h volume on a DEX (auto-checked).
  • If not detected, project may open a ticket with screenshot proof.
  • Visible spike in sentiment or activity may help qualify.

Verified

  • One-time fee of 300 CRO to initiate verification.
  • Reviewed by the Nodalis team manually.
  • We verify the following:
    • Official website
    • Whitepaper
    • Active social media accounts
    • Clear contact details
    • No scam reports from the community
    • Our AI audit tool screens the linked smart contracts
