🏅 Badges on Listings

Badges are visual indicators of a project’s performance, credibility, and engagement. They appear directly on each coin’s listing page and are earned through automated metrics and manual verification.

🏆 Current Badge Categories:

  • Innovative: Projects introducing new concepts or technology.
  • Top 1: Highest voted project on the platform.
  • Top 5 / Top 10: Based on highscore rankings.
  • Top Performer: High volume, community engagement, and growth.
  • Verified: Manually reviewed by Nodalis and confirmed as authentic.

More badges will roll out in the future to represent deeper metrics like transparency, roadmap milestones, and validator approval.

Badges help users instantly recognize trustworthy and trending projects.
