Nodalis is a community-powered listing platform for crypto projects, focused on transparency, reputation, and engagement.

Every project has a dedicated page with key data, community sentiment, badges, and performance.

Users can vote, follow, and engage with coins they support.

Projects can earn badges based on their credibility, activity, and popularity.

Nodalis helps users discover standout coins while rewarding projects for building trust.

The more active the community, the more visibility a project earns.
