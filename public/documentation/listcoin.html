🪙 How to List a Coin

Listing your coin on Nodalis is simple and free — but quality matters.

📋 Steps to List

  1. Navigate to the List a Coin page.
  2. Fill in the required details:
    • Token name, symbol, contract address
    • Project description
    • Website, socials, whitepaper, and logo
  3. Submit your listing for review.

🕒 After Submission

  • Your coin will be marked as “Pending”.
  • The Nodalis team will verify your project within 24–48 hours.
  • Once approved, your project becomes eligible for badges and community ranking.

🧩 Pro tip: Projects with more info and clear branding are more likely to earn early trust and engagement.
