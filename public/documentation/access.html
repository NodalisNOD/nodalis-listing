👉 Accessing the Platform

To fully experience Nodalis, from voting to interacting with projects, you’ll need to log in. We’ve made this super easy with Google Login via Firebase. No need to register or remember passwords.

🔓 How to log in:

  1. Click the “Login” button at the top right of the page.
  2. Select your Google account.
  3. Done! You’re instantly logged in and ready to go. 🚀

✅ Why log in?

  • Vote on community sentiment 💬
  • Vote in the Top Projects highscore 🏆
  • Access additional features as they roll out

🛡 Your identity is only used to ensure fair voting and prevent abuse. We do not store or share your personal Google data.

If you're logged out, voting and engagement buttons will be disabled and prompt you to sign in.
